Who we are

January Foundation is a grant-making foundation, supporting hardship grants for at-risk creatures big and small on land and sea. Our mission is to alleviate human suffering around the world by providing hardship grants to individuals, businesses and institutions. (Fill out our simple one-page form, no grant writing require)
